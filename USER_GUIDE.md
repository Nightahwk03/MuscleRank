# MuscleRank: The Ultimate Gamified Fitness Tracker
Welcome to **MuscleRank**, a next-generation workout tracker that gamifies your fitness journey by turning your real-life gains into RPG-style experience points! Level up your muscles, track your progress, build routines, and even pull rare collectible cards to reward your hard work.

This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know to become a MuscleRank master.

---

## 1. Getting Started & Profile Setup
MuscleRank runs directly in your browser and stores your data securely. When you first launch the app, your first goal is to set up your profile so the XP algorithms can accurately calibrate to your body.

1. **Navigate to the Profile & Settings Tab:** Located on the left navigation menu.
2. **Set Your Bodyweight:** This is **crucial**. MuscleRank calculates your Experience Points (XP) based on the ratio of the weight you lift compared to your own bodyweight.
3. **Select Your Unit:** Choose between Kilograms (kg) or Pounds (lbs). 
4. **Streak Multiplier:** As you consistently work out, your streak builds. A higher streak multiplier applies a bonus to all the XP you earn during your sessions!

---

## 2. Managing the Database (Masters)
Before you can log a workout, you need to tell the app what exercises you perform. MuscleRank gives you total freedom to build your own database.

### Exercise Master
*Found under the "Masters" section.*
- **Create an Exercise:** Click "Add New Exercise". Give it a name and specify whether it is a Compound movement (requires a warmup) or an Isolation movement.
- **Target Muscles:** Select the Primary muscle (receives 100% of the calculated XP) and any Secondary muscles (receives 30% of the calculated XP).
- **Bodyweight Exercises:** If you create an exercise like a Pull-up or Push-up, check the "Bodyweight" toggle. The algorithm will automatically add your profile's bodyweight to the calculations!
- **Edit & Delete:** You can easily update or remove exercises at any time.

### Workout Master (Routine Builder)
*Found under the "Masters" section.*
- **Create a Routine:** Group your exercises into saved templates (e.g., "Push Day", "Legs", "Full Body").
- **Set Minimum Sets:** Add exercises to your routine and define how many sets you plan to hit. If an exercise requires a warmup, you can even set the working sets to "0" to indicate it's purely a warmup block.
- **Total Sets Counter:** As you build your routine, a glowing purple counter will dynamically keep track of the total volume of sets for that day.

---

## 3. The Core Loop: Logging Your Workouts
*Found under the "Workout" section -> Record Session.*

1. **Start a Session:** Select one of your saved routines from the dropdown. 
2. **Log Your Sets:** For each exercise, enter the Weight and Reps you performed. 
3. **Warmup Sets:** If you check the "Warmup" box on a set, it will yield significantly less XP, preventing you from artificially inflating your stats with light weight.
4. **Save Draft:** Need to close the app mid-workout? Hit "Save as Draft." You can safely come back later and pick up exactly where you left off.
5. **Complete Workout:** When you finish, the MuscleRank engine calculates your total XP based on the weight ratio to your bodyweight, the rep ranges, and your streak multiplier. The XP is then distributed to the specific muscles targeted!

---

## 4. EXP, Ranks, and the Body Map
This is where the magic happens. MuscleRank turns your body into an RPG character.

*Found under the "Analysis" section -> Dashboard.*
- **The Ranking System:** Every muscle group (Chest, Lats, Quads, Biceps, etc.) starts at Rank E. As you feed them XP by logging sets, they will level up through the ranks: **E → D → C → B → A → S**.
- **Interactive Body Map:** Your dashboard features a beautiful, dynamic SVG map of the human body. As a muscle ranks up, it will visually change color on the map to reflect its new strength tier!
- **Hover Stats:** Hover over any muscle on the map to see its exact XP progress toward the next rank.

---

## 5. Analytics & Statistics
*Found under the "Analysis" section.*
- **Radar Chart:** A visual spider-web chart showing the balance of your physique. It helps you quickly identify lagging body parts (e.g., if your chest is Rank B but your back is Rank D).
- **Total Volume Tracking:** Track the sheer amount of weight you've moved over time, allowing you to ensure you are constantly applying progressive overload.

---

## 6. History and Auditing (Logs)
MuscleRank keeps a meticulous record of every action you take.

- **Workout Log:** View a chronological history of every workout you've ever completed, including the date, routine name, and total volume moved.
  - *Mistake?* If you accidentally logged a workout wrong, you can click the red **DELETE** button. MuscleRank will safely obliterate the log, recalculate the XP it granted, deduct it from your muscles, and even demote your muscle ranks back down if necessary!
- **Change Log (Audit Trail):** A silent ledger that tracks administrative actions. If you create, edit, or delete an exercise from your master database, or delete a workout, it is permanently recorded here for your review.

---

## 7. The Reward System: Pack Pulls
To keep you motivated, MuscleRank features a built-in minigame reward system!

- **Pack Pull:** Spend your hard-earned workout currency to rip open virtual booster packs. Enjoy a beautifully animated sequence that reveals rare collectible cards!
- **Pull Log (Lightbox Gallery):** View your entire collection of pulled cards. Click on any card to enlarge it into a stunning premium gallery view. You can use the on-screen `<` and `>` arrows, or your keyboard arrow keys, to seamlessly browse through your collection.
